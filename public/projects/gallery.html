3D Asset Gallery

Digital Content Creation for Project KALPIT: Gamification Based on Indian Ethos.

These assets were developed as part of Project KALPIT (Digital Creation to Enable Gamification Based on Indian Ethos) at the Center for Visual Intelligence (CEVI), KLE Technological University. The project focuses on building a culturally-rooted gaming ecosystem by reconstructing high-fidelity 3D assets from historical references and traditional designs using AI-driven pipelines.
