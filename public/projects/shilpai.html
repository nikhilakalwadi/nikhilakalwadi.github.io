ShilpAI: 3D Asset Generator

An in-house generative tool for Indian Ethos-based 3D assets.

ShilpAI is developed as a core component of Project KALPIT (Digital Creation to Enable Gamification Based on Indian Ethos) at the Center for Visual Intelligence (CEVI). The tool automates the creation of high-fidelity 3D assets from cultural artifacts, traditional jewelry, and historical references, enabling the rapid population of immersive digital environments with authentic Indian content.

ShilpAI leverages cutting-edge generative AI to bridge the gap between 2D conceptual designs and 3D digital assets. It allows researchers and creators to transform a single photograph or even a text description into a fully realized 3D model with Physically-Based Rendering (PBR) materials, optimized for real-time applications.

Technical Pipeline

The pipeline is largely inspired by state-of-the-art generative models like TRELLIS and Hunyuan 3D 2.1, customized to handle the intricate geometries and material properties common in Indian artifacts and jewelry.

  • Geometry Extraction: High-resolution surface reconstruction from sparse inputs.
  • Material Synthesis: Automated PBR map generation (Albedo, Normal, Roughness, Metallic) for realistic lighting.
  • Topological Optimization: Intelligent mesh simplification to ensure mobile-ready performance in AR/VR frameworks.

Key Features

Single Image to 3D

Direct 3D reconstruction from a single photograph or concept art, optimized for intricate jewelry details.

Multi-view to 3D

High-fidelity reconstruction using multiple input angles to ensure geometric accuracy across all surfaces.

Text to 3D

Prompt-based asset generation allowing for rapid prototyping of creative jewelry designs.

Image to Multi-view Pipeline

Advanced stage that generates consistent multi-view perspectives from a single image to refine the final 3D synthesis.

Interactive 3D Viewer

Real-time inspection of generated models with full PBR material support and dynamic lighting.

GLB Export

One-click downloads in industry-standard formats for seamless integration into AR/VR and game engines.

Asset Customization

Post-generation tools to fine-tune materials, scale, and textures to match specific design requirements.
